Everything Else

On the opposite end of the spectrum, I am very involved in the music scene at Oberlin College. We're called Metalmarks, and we make a variety of music, from math rock to shoegaze to grunge. Also, I am the Social Media manager of the Oberlin Gear Co-op. The Gear Co-op provides a practice space for non-conservatory students, as well as renting out equipment to students for concerts and house shows.
